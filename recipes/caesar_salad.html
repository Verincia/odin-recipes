Caesar Salad

Description

A leafy salad that consists of delectable romaine lettuce and other toppings to have a nice side dish.

Ingredients

  • caesar dressing
  • romaine lettuce
  • croutons
  • parmesan cheese

Cooking Directions

  1. cut the romaine lettuce into a bowl
  2. place the croutons and the cheese on top of the lettuce
  3. drip the dressing onto the salad
  4. enjoy
