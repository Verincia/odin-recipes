Jambalaya

Description

An easy to cook meal that can bring a bit of zaz to your evening.

ingredients

    one box of jambalaya mix ground turkey water

Cooking Directions

    cook the ground turkey mix 2 1/2 cups of water, jambalaya mix, and turkey in 3 quart saucepan until well blended bring to a boil reduce heat to a low cover, simmer for 25 minutes or until rice is tender Remove from heat, let stand for 5 minutes
