Jambalaya

Description

An easy to cook meal that can bring a bit of zaz to your evening.

ingredients

  • one box of jambalaya mix
  • ground turkey
  • water

Cooking Directions

  1. cook the ground turkey
  2. mix 2 1/2 cups of water, jambalaya mix, and turkey in 3 quart saucepan until well blended
  3. bring to a boil
  4. reduce heat to a low
  5. cover, simmer for 25 minutes or until rice is tender
  6. Remove from heat, let stand for 5 minutes
